Encinal Music Boosters

What are the boosters?

The Boosters are the Families, Friends and Supporters of our Fantastic Encinal Music Program - Advanced Marching-/Symphonic Band, Orchestra and Jazz Band. The Boosters have worked hard to maintain a non-profit status and have a Board made up of Band parents volunteers. The Boosters meet on the first Wednesday of every month at 7pm during the school year. Meetings take place at Encinal High School in the Band Room - click here for a complete schedule.

What do the boosters do?

As a non-profit, we are able to partner with community members to fundraise much needed capital for our cash-strapped program. This includes putting together Spaghetti Dinners, organizing the Band Review, Hosting Play-a-thons and more. 100% of all money raised goes towards the music program expenses such as instrument repair, uniform cleaning, bus rental and more.

How can you help?

We are always in need of donations. If you are able to donate time, money, water or anything, we welcome it. Please contact the Booster President, Richard Hall, at encinalband@gmail.com to find out how you can contribute to this award winning program.

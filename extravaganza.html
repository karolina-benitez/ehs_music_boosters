Some of the streets surrounding Encinal High School will be closed for part of the day during the event. They are as follows:


  • Central Avenue- 100 and 200 block (including boat ramp access)

  • Haight Avenue - 300 block
  • 3rd Street - between Central Ave. and Pacific Ave. (1400 - 1700 block)
